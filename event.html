Code Buddy

A competitive coding competition based on understanding and decoding codes with speed and continue the code of your code buddy.

1st round:
This will be a fast coding round where 10 easy questions will be given and your team need to code and pass all the test cases in 30 min. Platform is Hacker Rank and language allowed is C/C++/Java.
2nd Round:
  1. Second round is a coding round where on 2 different PCs you and your team partner will code different questions.
  2. After time interval of 15 mins, you will be swapping places with your partner and need to complete his code.
  3. Swapping will be done for 3 times,ie, four 15 mins slots are there.
  4. So try to CODE with your CodeBuddy understanding and decoding his logic !
Rules and Regulations-
  1. 1. Each team will consist of 2 members.
  2. 2. Team members aren't allowed to communicate with each other during the contest.
  3. 3. Use of Mobile phones,internet or any other electronic device is prohibited.
  4. 4. Any type of comments in the code will lead to disqualification of the team.
  5. 5. The contest duration is 60 minutes and the partners are required to exchange their seats at midtime.
  6. 6. The decision of judges will be final and binding on all participants.

Team Distribution-
  1. FE/SE (Junior Team): Team of 2
  2. TE/BE (Senior Team): Team of 2

Bug Off

Bug Off is a one of its kind coding competition where the participants are expected to remove the bugs from the given set of codes and achieve the desired output within the time limit. The participant (Team/Individual) with maximum number of error-free codes wins the contest.

Rules and Regulations-
  1. 1. Both the groups, FE-SE and TE-BE, will be given maximum 60 minutes to compete.
  2. 2. FE-SE participants will be given a choice between C/C++/JAVA languages while TE-BE participants won't be having any such choice.
  3. 3. The participants of FE-SE will be allowed to use the compiler for an interval of 5 minutes in every 20 minutes.
  4. 4. The participants of TE-BE will be allowed to use the compiler for an interval of 10 minutes after every 20 minutes.
  5. 5. Use of compilers in the time span other than the provided time interval will lead to disqualification of the team.
  6. 6. Round 1 consists of Bugged codes which participants need to Debug in any order,while in round 2 solving questions in given order is mandatory.There won't be anyskips for questions in round 2.
